Create a bounty

Commit — seal your answer

Your answer is hashed locally with a random salt, your address and the bounty id. Only the hash goes on-chain — nobody can read it, and copying your hash is useless to anyone else.

Save this salt — you need it to reveal

Reveal — open the seal

After submissions close, send the exact answer and salt. The contract recomputes the hash and accepts only a perfect match. If you committed from this browser, both fields prefill automatically.

Judge & finalize — owner only

One batch inference: the rubric and every revealed answer go into a single Ritual LLM call (precompile 0x0802). The AI recommends; you finalize. Inference fees are escrowed from your RitualWallet balance (~0.31 RITUAL per call).
